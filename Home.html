Our Process


A time-tested process that helps us deliver impact


Avriti is a process-centric company. We believe that a well-thought process is what leads to delivering high-quality work. From ideation until delivery, we follow a four-step process that never fails.


Discovery


The first phase where we take a deep dive into the problems your business is facing. We approach the problems from multiple angles and stakeholder views to understand the right approach to solve them. A technology-agnostic approach is taken at this juncture to ensure that maximum emphasis is given to formulating a solution than deciding a factor which leads to discovery.


Ideation


Once the pain points are identified and mapped to respective stakeholders, we go about brainstorming plausible ideas that can be explored as appropriate solutions. This is also the stage where ideal technologies and the experiences that they provide are considered to solve the stakeholder problems. The idea is approached from a creative and logical point of view to ensure that it is efficient as well as engaging to the end-user.

Development


The third stage where the finalized solution and the technology are given a solid shape. We use elaborate project management methodologies to build mockups, prototypes, and also beta versions that demonstrate how the final solution will work. Avriti has a team of developers, QA professionals, and creative brains to ensure that the solution is well-rounded in all aspects.


Deployment


Deployment is the final stage after QA testing and refinements where we deploy the completed product in the relevant platform. We help you launch mobile apps in app stores, launch VR and AR applications on mobile or websites, and go the extra mile to ensure that your business objectives are met with the final deployment.
